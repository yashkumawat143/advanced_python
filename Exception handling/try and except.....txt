1....Write a program to divide two numbers entered by the user.
answer :

try:
    n1=int(input("Enter the number : "));
    n2=int(input("Enter the number do you want to divide : "));
    result=n1/n2;
    print("result : ",result);
except ZeroDivisionError:
    print("It is not divisible");
